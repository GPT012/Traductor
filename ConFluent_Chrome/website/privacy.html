Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

ConFluent ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension handles your data.

1. Data Collection

ConFluent is designed with privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account to use the extension.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your browsing history or personal usage patterns.
  • No Database: We do not have a database storing your messages or translations.

2. Data Usage

The extension only processes data locally on your device to perform its core function: translation.

  • Text Translation: When you type text or view messages, the extension sends this specific text snippet to a translation API (such as Google Translate API) solely for the purpose of returning the translated text.
  • Ephemeral Processing: This data is transient and is not stored by ConFluent after the translation is complete.

3. Permissions

We request the minimum permissions necessary for the extension to function:

  • "Read and change all your data on the websites you visit" (Host Permissions): This sound scary, but it is strictly required to detect the text you type into input fields on websites (like Discord, WhatsApp, etc.) so we can replace it with the translation. We do not read anything else.
  • Storage: Used only to save your local settings (e.g., your preferred target language) on your own device.

4. Third-Party Services

ConFluent utilizes third-party translation APIs to provide translations. These services may process the text you submit for translation according to their own privacy policies.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@confluents.xyz
